## 📌 PARA Framework
PARA stands for:  
  
- Projects  
- Areas  
- Resources  
- Archives  
  
It is an organizational method created by Tiago Forte.  
  
---
## Projects  
Short-term efforts with a clear outcome.  
  
---  
  
## Areas  
Long-term responsibilities with no end date.  
  
---  
  
## Resources  
Reference material that supports Projects or Areas.  
  
---  
  
## Archives  
Inactive or completed items.  
  
---  
  
## Why It Matters In This Vault  
  
This vault is PARA-inspired:  
  
- `Notes/Projects` → Active efforts  
- `Notes/Areas` → Ongoing life domains  
- `Notes/Resources` → Reference material  
- `Archives` → Completed or inactive  
  
The system organizes by actionability, not topic.
